# Thumbnail Builder Design Specifications

## 1. Color Palette

### Backgrounds
- **Deep Space Black**: `#050508` (Main app background)
- **Blueprint Grid**: `#0A0A12` (Grid lines on background)
- **Surface Card**: `#12121A` (Content containers)
- **Surface Hover**: `#1A1A24`

### Accents (Neon DNA)
- **Rocket Orange**: `#FF5500` (Primary CTA, Active States)
- **Electric Purple**: `#9D00FF` (Gradients, Secondary Highlights)
- **Cyber Blue**: `#00D4FF` (Tech accents, Info)
- **Success Green**: `#00FF94`
- **Error Red**: `#FF0044`

### Text
- **Primary**: `#FFFFFF`
- **Secondary**: `#9494A8`
- **Tertiary**: `#58586A`

## 2. Typography

### Font Family
- **Primary**: `Inter` or `Outfit` (Modern, clean sans-serif)

### Styles
- **Display Headline**: 48px, Bold, Tight tracking
- **Section Title**: 24px, SemiBold
- **Label**: 12px, Uppercase, Wide tracking (1.5px)
- **Body**: 14px, Regular, 150% line height

## 3. Component Styles

### Cards
- **Shape**: Rounded corners (12px or 16px)
- **Border**: 1px solid `#FFFFFF10` (Subtle white outline)
- **Effect**: Inner glow on hover (Orange/Purple gradient)
- **Shadow**: Large, soft colored drop shadows for active elements

### Buttons
- **Primary**: Gradient Orange to Red-Orange, Rounded full, White text, Glow effect.
- **Secondary**: Transparent with White border, White text.
- **Ghost**: Text only, Orange hover.

### Inputs
- **Background**: `#08080C`
- **Border**: `#FFFFFF20`
- **Focus**: `#FF5500` Border + Glow

## 4. Visual Assets
- **Logo**: Neon line art (Camera/Face/Play), Gradient stroke.
- **Icons**: Thin line stroke (1.5px), Neon colors.
- **Imagery**: Realistic face photos, high-quality thumbnails, no "cartoon" 3D unless specified.
